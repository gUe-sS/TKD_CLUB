ABOUT US

Among the large variety of sports facilities offered at IIT Kanpur, also lies the opportunity to learn the historic defense art form of Taekwondo. Taekwondo isn't just about physical strength but also about concentration and self-control. In IIT Kanpur, Taekwondo is organised by Taekwondo Club ,commonly known as TKD club. Students learn Taekwondo under the guidance of the instructor Balram Yadav (Dan 5 Black Belt). A lot of importance is given to discipline and focus. It is not just an exercise, but development of a complete personality. It teaches a lot about building self-control and respect, not just for friends but also for opponents. Taekwondo performance is a mixture of steadiness and swiftness. It takes place at Taekwondo Arena, main stadium, near volleyball court. Our main focus is to provide a best physical fitness, competitive atmosphere to absorbed as a sport and self-defence specific skill which may come handy at the time of trouble .
